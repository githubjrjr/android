About The White Dress film

Legend has it that if you make eye contact with the little girl lurking around Jurong Lake Gardens, you would get cursed with bad luck. Despite the warning, Michelle, a new resident, decides to investigate and encounters Alyssa, the girl from the legend. Alyssa shares that she lost her childhood friend and comes to the gardens to fulfill their bucket list. Michelle decides to help Alyssa complete the list, and they develop a close friendship. As they explore the neighborhood and gardens together, they gain a deeper understanding of the area and find solace in their shared experiences.
